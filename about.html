About Vaultstone

Banking Built on
Conviction

Founded in 1998 by former Goldman Sachs executives with a singular belief: every individual and business deserves access to the same institutional tools once reserved for the ultra-wealthy.

Our Mission

We Believe Finance Should Serve People

For too long, institutional-quality financial services were gated behind minimum balances and private client thresholds. Vaultstone was built to dismantle that gate — permanently.

Today, with $48B AUM, 2.4 million clients, and operations in 147 countries, we remain privately held and obsessively focused on one metric: client outcomes. Not quarterly earnings. Not shareholder returns. Client outcomes.

What Drives Us

Four Pillars of Vaultstone

Client-First, Always
Every product decision, every policy, every line of code is evaluated through a single lens: does this make our clients financially stronger? If the answer isn't an unambiguous yes, we don't ship it.
Radical Transparency
No hidden fees. No confusing fine print. No conflicts of interest. We earn revenue when our clients earn returns — full stop. Our fee structures are published, plain-English, and audited annually.
Engineering Excellence
We invest 22% of revenue in R&D — 3x the industry average. Our infrastructure runs at 99.99% uptime, and our security posture is continuously validated by external red-team exercises.
Global Inclusion
Whether you're in Lagos, Lahore, or London, you deserve the same product quality and support. We localize for 87 languages, offer 34 currency accounts, and hire locally in every major region we serve.

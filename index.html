About Vaultstone

We Believe Banking Should Serve You

Founded in 1998 by former Goldman Sachs executives, Vaultstone was built on a simple conviction: individuals and businesses deserve the same institutional-grade tools that were once reserved for the ultra-wealthy.

Today, with $48B AUM and 2.4 million clients across 147 countries, we remain privately held, debt-free, and obsessively focused on one metric — client outcomes.

Our Culture

We Work Differently.
Intentionally.

Vaultstone isn't a bank that happens to have good culture — it's a culture that happens to build a great bank. Here's what that means day-to-day.

Remote-First, Always

We've been remote-first since day one. No performative office mandates — just async-friendly workflows, documented decisions, and trust in our people to do their best work anywhere.

Radical Ownership

Every person owns their domain end-to-end — from ideation to production. No hand-off culture, no diffusion of responsibility. If you build it, you own its success.

High-Signal Communication

We write decisions down, keep meetings ruthlessly optional, and default to written proposals over endless syncs. Your time and attention are protected as company resources.

Psychological Safety

We disagree loudly in private and support decisions publicly. Speaking up is never political risk here — the best idea wins regardless of title or tenure.

Growth That's Real

$5,000 annual learning budget, internal mobility encouraged, weekly "deep work" time protected on Fridays. We invest in your growth because our clients benefit from it.

Exceptional Compensation

Top-of-market salaries benchmarked quarterly, meaningful equity, and transparent pay bands. No negotiation penalty — everyone with the same role and experience earns the same.

How We Hire

A Process That Respects Your Time

No take-home projects that consume your weekend. No 8-round loops. Here's what to expect from first contact to offer.

1
Application Review
We review every application within 5 business days. No black holes — you'll always hear back.
2

3
